> 作为一款小体量的 AVG 游戏，《灵视异闻 FILE38 伊势人鱼物语》全结局时长大概在 20 小时左右，确实相比前作要多了 1.5 倍，虽然价格也因此略有上涨（500 日元左右），但得到的体验也同样没有打折，同样是关于诅咒的故事，FILE38 以动人凄美的主题，诠释了爱的理念，使其展现出与 FILE23 不同的优秀剧本。

As a small-scale Adventure Game (AVG), "PARANORMASIGHT: The Mermaid's Curse FILE 38" all in all takes about 20 hours to complete, clearly 1.5 times longer than its predecessor. While it's tag price has thus also increased (about 500 yen), the experience gained from it isn't shortchanged. Its story is about a curse with a beautiful, moving theme that interprets the concept of love in a way that makes FILE38 an excellent drama like no other.
