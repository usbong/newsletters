Spain advances to the semi-finals, winning against Belgium despite losing its first point in this year's FIFA World Cup 

> Translator: [Michael B. Syson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelsyson/)

> Publication Date: 2026-07-11

## --

During the FIFA World Cup this July 10th (Japan Time: July 11), Spain crushes Belgium with a 2-1 on the 29th day of the quarter-finals. 

After Spain led during most of the first half, Belgium eventually caught up, ending with a 1-1. While Spain lost its first point in this year's world cup, Merino was able to score the deciding goal at 43 minutes into the second half.

The quarter-finals between Norway against England and Argentina against Switzerland will be on July 11th (Japan Time: July 12).

Spain will be battling against France in the semi-finals on the 14th (Japan Time: July 15).
