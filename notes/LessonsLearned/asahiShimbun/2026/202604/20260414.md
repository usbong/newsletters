> 駅そば、地域に愛され復活の予感　ブランド統一方針のJRも個性共存

Bringing back memories of regional affection through the soba noodle shops at the train station, JR Railways going for individual coexistence with its brand unification policy 

> 2026年4月14日 6時00分

2026-04-14T06:00

> 岡本智

Satoshi OKAMOTO

## --

> 　時代の流れとともに閉店が相次いだ「駅そば」が復活したり、実は根強く残ったりしている――。こんな話を取材先から聞き、奈良県内の高校に通った大阪出身の記者は腹をすかせては駅でかき込んだ、きつねうどんを懐かしく思い出した。駅そばの現在地を探るべく、いま居を構える栃木県を中心に、北関東のあちらこちらに食べに行ってみた。

As time goes by, the many "soba noodle shops at the train station" that have close down one by one are brought back, having been actually strongly rooted in the place. After hearing this news story from his respondents, the author, who was born in Osaka and went to a high school in Nara prefecture, became hungry and went to the train station, after feeling a longing and craving for the kitsune udon noodles. He had to look for the place where the soba noodle shops at the train station are now, because he now mainly lives in Tochigi prefecture, though he's tried to go to various places in northern Kanto region to dine.

>　茨城県筑西市と栃木県茂木町を結ぶ真岡鉄道の真岡駅（同県真岡市）の改札近くにある、1年半ほど前に開店した立ち食いそば「しりゅう」。4月上旬の昼下がり、部活動帰りとおぼしき女子高校生が財布の小銭をかき集め、学生ラーメン（390円）と揚げたてのコロッケ（100円）を注文した。

Near the ticket gate of Mooka Railway's Mooka Station (in Mooka City), a place that connects Chikusei City in Ibaraki Prefecture with Motegi Town in Tochigi Prefecture, the soba noodle shop called "SHIRYU" was opened about a year and half ago. In the afternoon of early April, coming home from school club activities, a high school student-looking girl put together loose change from her wallet and ordered the student-discounted ramen (390 yen) and the freshly fried croquette (100 yen).
