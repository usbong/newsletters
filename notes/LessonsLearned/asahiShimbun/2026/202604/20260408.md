> 高市首相、石油供給「年を越えて確保にめど」　補正予算には慎重姿勢

PM Takaichi secures over a year's worth of oil supply, while revising the budget, in a precarious situation 

> 2026年4月7日 21時23分

2026-04-07T21:23

> 田嶋慶彦

Yoshihiko Tajima

## --

> 高市早苗首相は7日、首相官邸で記者団の取材に応じ、イラン情勢の悪化に伴う石油の安定供給への懸念について「年を越えて石油の供給を確保できるめどがついた」と述べた。また、中東情勢の影響を見通すことは困難とし、現時点では補正予算の編成に否定的な考えを示した。

Prime Minister Sanae Takaichi spoke during a press conference at the PM's office on April 7th about having "secured over a year's worth of oil supply," given the worsening situation in Iran, affecting the stability of procuring oil. Further, since it's difficult to predict the impact of the situation in the Middle East, she expressed a negative view toward revising the budget at the present time.

> 首相は「日本全体として必要となる量は確保されている」と言及。原油について中東、米国などからの「代替調達」を増やし、備蓄分も含めて当面は対応できると説明した。今後についても「代替調達率をさらに引き上げるべく、産油国への働きかけを強化する」と語った。

The PM mentioned, "Japan as a whole has secured the necessary amounts." With regard to oil, Japan has increased "procurement from alternative sources" such as the US and the Middle East. She explained that they can meet the target by also including Japan's current reserves. She said, "We must further raise the ratio of procuring oil from alternative sources, while strengthening our efforts aimed at oil-producing countries."
