> 人工知能（AI）やアンドロイドが、人とともに生きる世界を描いた漫画・アニメの企画展「攻殻機動隊展 Ghost and the Shell」が、虎ノ門ヒルズ（東京都港区）の情報発信拠点「TOKYO NODE」で開かれている。37年前の原作だが、現代を想起させる進んだ科学技術と人間の本質に迫る物語が魅力で、訪日客を含めた世界のファンを引きつけている。

The special exhibit of the "Ghost and the Shell," a manga/anime that depicts a world where AI, Androids and humans live together, has been opened at the "TOKYO NODE," an information hub, in Tora no Mon Hills (Tokyo Minato District). While the masterpiece began 37 years ago, it still attracts fans from all over the world including visitors to Japan due to its fascinating story that penetrates into the core essence of both human beings and advanced science and technology that is reminiscent of modern times.
