Mr. Yuji Horii, the Father of the Dragon Quest series, launched in 1983 what would become his first big hit called "Portopia Serial Murder Case," an adventure game whose name is linked to "Future City," Kobe City's artificial island that was completed two years earlier. Why did Mr. Yuji Horii name his game "Portopia?" We ask him about his recollections on its creation during that period.
