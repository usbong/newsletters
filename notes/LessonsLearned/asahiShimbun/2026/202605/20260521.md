Chef Ms. Kimiko Hiyamizu responded to us at the editorial committee and our readers about a longtime dream of ours to come up with a series where she'd cook food. This time around, her dish makes use of chicken breast with ingredients that are gentle on the pocket. While chicken breast easily dries up when cooked, by adding egg batter in order to make the picatta, the dish can be made to be moist and juicy.
