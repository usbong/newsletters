> ドイツ移民の料理をきっかけに、19世紀半ばに誕生

Inspired by the dishes of German immigrants, the hamburger was born in the mid-19th century

>　世界各地から移民が集まる米国は、料理も各国の伝統をくむ。そんな中、米国生まれの「国民食」と言えば、間違いなくハンバーガーだろう。

As a nation where immigrants from many countries gather, America has also inherited food traditions from these countries. Among these traditions, there's a dish known to be the "National Dish." Born in America, it's none other than the hamburger.

>　牛のひき肉をこねた料理は、ドイツからの移民が持ち込んだとされる。港町・ハンブルクにちなんで、米国では「ハンブルク・ステーキ」と呼ばれていたという。

Dishes made from ground beef were said to have been brought by immigrants from Germany. In America, these were said to have been called "Hamburg Steak," in honor of the port of Hamburg.
