> Spain wins the semi-finals against France 2-0, advances to the finals for the first time after four World Cups 

> Writer: [Michael B. Syson](https://www.linkedin.com/in/michaelsyson/)

> Publication Date: 2026-07-15T05:21 (Updated: 2026-07-15T06:06)

## --

During the World Cup on July 14th (Japan Time: July 15), the tournament's 31th day, Spain wins the semi-finals against France 2-0, advancing to the finals for the first time after four World Cup tournaments.

During the first half and just past 20 minutes into the game, Spain's Yamal was awarded a penalty kick after falling inside the penalty area. Oyarzabal clinched the goal and took the first point.

Even during the second half, Spain controlled the ball, with Porro adding a point 13 minutes into the game.

France, which is the runner-up in the lasts World Cup tournament, hasn't been able to win the championship for three consecutive tournaments.

The other semi-finals match is on July 15th (Japan Time: 16th) and will be a fight between Argentina and England.
