Adding a combination of sourness, sweetness and salt makes the Filipino dish taste like Japanese food

## --

>　フィリピン料理　フィリピンの主食はコメで、日本人にもなじみやすい。代表的な調味料はスーカ（酢）、パティス（魚醤（ぎょしょう））、トヨ（しょうゆ）、バゴーン（発酵調味料）の四つで、酸味や甘味、塩味をかけ合わせて味のバランスをとるという。フィリピン政府観光省のウェブサイト（[https://philippinetravel.jp/filipino-food](https://philippinetravel.jp/filipino-food)/別ウインドウで開きます）で、代表的な料理のレシピが紹介されている。

Filipino food is mainly accompanied with rice, so it's easy for Japanese people to become used to it. The four best-known condiments are suka (vinegar), patis (fish sauce), toyo (soy sauce) and bagoong (fermented paste). It's said that a balance in the food's taste can be achieved by adding a combination of sourness, sweetness and salt into the dish. The recipes of famous dishes are introduced on the Philippine government's tourism website: [https://philippinetravel.jp/filipino-food/](https://philippinetravel.jp/filipino-food/)
