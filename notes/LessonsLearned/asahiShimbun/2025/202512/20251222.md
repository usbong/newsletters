>　女王アリの巣乗っ取り方法は「母殺し」だった　愛好家らが新たに発見

The way to take over the queen ant's nest is "to kill the mother," a new discovery made by ant enthusiasts

> 中村有紀子

Yukiko Nakamura 

> 2025年12月21日 17時00分

2025-12-21T17:00

## --

>　働きアリたちに実母の女王アリを殺させて巣を乗っ取る――。「寄生アリ」のそんな寄生先での女王アリ排除方法を、九州大学などの研究チームが新たに発見した。きっかけは、アリ愛好家の粘り強い観察。地中にいる女王アリの実態解明は容易ではなく、九州大の研究者はこの貴重な発見について「アリを酔狂のように愛している人がなせる業」と話す。

The worker children of the queen ant are made to kill their actual mother so that an intruder, another queen ant, could take over the nest. This way of killing the queen ant by another queen ant was newly discovered by the research team at Kyushu University, among others. The keen observation of ant enthusiasts has led researchers to an invaluable discovery. They say that it's "a job that can only be done by crazy ant enthusiasts," especially because bringing to light the queen ant's actual circumstance inside the soil isn't easy.

>　学術誌「カレント・バイオロジー」に11月17日付で論文が掲載された（https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cub.2025.09.037別ウインドウで開きます）。九州大の高須賀圭三助教（生態学）によると、寄生者が自身の利益になる行動を寄生先に強制する「寄主操作」のなかで、子を操って母殺しをさせる現象は生物学史上前例がないという。

The paper was published last November 17 in the academic journal, "Current Biology" (https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cub.2025.09.037; last accessed: 20251222). According to Assist. Prof. Keizo Takasuka (Ecology), there's no prior example in the history of biology about controlling the children and making them kill their mother through a kind of "parasitic control" that is forced upon them by another queen ant for the sake of her own benefit. 

>　アリの巣では、女王アリが働きアリを産み続けている。寄生アリの中には、他種の巣に女王アリが忍び込んで寄生先の女王アリを排除し、働きアリに自分の子どもを育てさせる種がいることが知られている。

The queen ant is known to continuously give birth to worker ants in her nest. However, it's been found that there's a type of queen ant that kills another queen ant by sneaking into her nest, and then raising her own children in that nest as worker ants.

>　しかし排除方法についてはほぼ未解明で、寄生アリの女王が寄生先の女王アリの首をかんで切り落とす方法のみが知られていた。

This method of killing the queen ant was hardly known prior to this discovery. Previously, the only known way was for the queen ant to bite off the neck of the other queen ant whose nest she wants to take over.

>　新たな排除法を発見したのは、アリの通販専門店「アントルーム」（東京都）代表の島田拓さん。2021年8月、当時9歳だった長男の永久（とわ）さん（14）と山梨県の山でアリ採集をしていた。その際に、永久さんが地面の上で偶然見つけたのが、普段は地中の巣にいる女王アリ。寄生先の女王を排除するテラニシクサアリだった。

Mr. Taku Shimada from the Mail Order Ant Specialty Store, "Ant Room," in Tokyo discovered this new method of taking over another queen ant's nest. In August 2021, he had gone with Mr. Towa, his eldest son who was 9 years old at the time (now 14), to the mountains of Yamanashi Prefecture in order to collect ants. During that time, Mr. Towa chanced upon a queen ant on the open surface of the soil. Normally, the queen ant is in her nest inside the soil; however, what he found was a Lasius orientalis queen ant, the type that kills other queen ants.

> 島田さんのブログに研究者「驚愕のあまり絶句」

Researchers were "so shocked that they were speechless" when they saw this discovery on Mr. Shimada's blog.
