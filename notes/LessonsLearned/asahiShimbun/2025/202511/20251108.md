>　DNAの二重らせん構造の発見者の一人の米科学者、ジェームズ・ワトソン氏が7日、米ニューヨーク州イースト・ノースポートのホスピスで死去した。97歳だった。米紙ニューヨーク・タイムズなどが伝えた。死因は明らかになっていないが、同紙は息子の話として、最近感染症の治療を受けていたとした。

American scientist and discoverer of the DNA's Double Helix Structure James Watson passed away on November 7th at the East Northport Hospice in New York. He was 97 years old. U.S. paper New York Times and others conveyed the news. While the cause of his death hasn't yet been revealed, the New York Times reported that based on a conversation with the son of the deceased, Mr. Watson had recently been undergoing treatment for an infectious disease.

> 1928年、シカゴ生まれ。英ケンブリッジ大学で故フランシス・クリック博士とともに研究し、DNAが2本の鎖がより合わさったらせん階段のような構造であることを発見し、53年に論文で発表。20世紀の生物学における最大の成果と言われ、62年にはノーベル生理学・医学賞を受けた。

Mr. Watson was born in Chicago in 1928. He conducted research work with Dr. Francis Crick (deceased) at Cambridge University in the U.K., discovering the Double Helix Structure of the DNA and publishing their discovery in a thesis in 1953. Recognized as the greatest discovery in the field of Biology in the 20th century, they received the Nobel Prize for Biology and Medicine in 1962.

In 2007, Mr. Watson made a pronouncement that caused a big commotion. He said that the gist of human discrimination is that black people are racially inferior. Even after this incident, he again made the same pronouncement and was criticized.
