> ソフトバンクグループ（SBG）が11日発表した2025年9月中間決算（国際会計基準）は、最終的なもうけを示す純利益が2兆9240億円で前年同期（1兆53億円）の2.9倍となり、中間決算での過去最高を更新した。保有する米オープンAI株の評価額が大きく上昇した。米半導体大手エヌビディアの全株式を約9千億円で売却したことも明らかにした。

In the mid-term financial report (International Accounting Standards) presented by SoftBank Group on November 11th, it showed that it earned a final net profit of 2.9 trillion yen, an increase of 2.9 times from the previous year during the same period (1.5 trillion yen), thereby becoming the highest that the group has ever achieved in record history. 

The value of the shares that it owns of OpenAI has risen greatly, and it announced that it's sold all its shares of semiconductor giant Nvidia, amounting to a total of about 9 trillion yen.
