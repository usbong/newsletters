> 精神健康往往不是被一件大事击垮，而是被日常“小习惯”慢慢掏空：熬夜、屏幕刷不停、对自己太苛刻、从不真正休息。职能治疗师提醒，别低估这些行为带来的伤害；只要在日常做出小改变，就有机会为精神健康“止损”，找回生活的平衡。

Oftentimes, mental health isn't one big defeat, but a "small habit" done daily to gradually empty ourselves from things like staying up late at night, scrolling through the screen non-stop, being too hard on oneself and never really taking a break. An occupational therapist reminds us to not belittle the harm that these actions bring. By making small changes in our daily life, we have the opportunity to "stop the harm" that they bring to our mental health, and get back the balance in our lives.
