XI, Jinping's 2026 New Year Address: Breakthrough in Independent Chip R&D, Movement toward National Unification Unstoppable

> 发布/2025年12月31日 20:32

Published: 2025-12-31T20:32

## --

> 中国国家主席习近平在新年前夕发表2026年新年贺词时说，中国晶片自主研发有了新突破，中国成为创新力上升最快的经济体之一。他也向对岸喊话，强调两岸人民血浓于水，国家统一的历史大势不可阻挡。

During New Year's Eve, President of China XI, Jinping said in his 2026 New Year Address that China has achieved a breakthrough in independent Chip R&D, and that China has become one of the fastest growing economies in the field of innovation. He also yelled toward the people on the other side of the Taiwan Strait, emphasizing that the blood that ties both peoples on each side of the strait is thicker than water, and that historical movement toward national unification cannot be stopped.
