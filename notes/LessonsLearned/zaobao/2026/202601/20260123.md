> 特朗普：习近平今年底访美 期待与他见面

Trump: XI, Jinping is visiting the US at the end of the year, I look forward to meeting with him

> 发布/2026年1月23日 07:07

Published: 2026-01-23T07:07

## Last updated: 2026-01-24T13:59

## --

> 美国总统特朗普说，他4月将访问中国，中国国家主席习近平将于今年年底访问美国。

US President Trump says that he's visiting China in April, while President of China XI, Jinping is visiting the US at the end of the year.

> 特朗普星期四（1月22日）在空军一号上对记者说：“我期待见到习近平主席，我一直与中国国家主席习近平保持着良好的关系。”

Last Thursday (Jan. 22), Trump told reporters inside Air Force One, "I look forward to seeing President XI, Jinping. I always keep good relations with China's President, XI, Jinping."

> 特朗普说，冠病疫情期间，中美关系一度紧张，但此后已显著改善。他还说，中国正在大量购买美国大豆，这对美国农民来说是件好事。

Trump said that the relationship between the US and China became strained during the COVID-19 pandemic, though it has significantly improved afterward. He also says that China is currently buying large amounts of US soybeans, a good thing for US farmers.
