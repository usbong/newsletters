> 特朗普对梅洛尼态度转变 称“令人不可接受”

Trump says Meloni "makes people unable to accept her," changing his attitude toward her

> 发布/2026年4月15日 14:28

Published: 2026-04-15T14:28

## --

> 美国总统特朗普抨击他曾经的政治盟友——意大利总理梅洛尼，“不在乎伊朗是否拥有核武器”，“令人不可接受”。特朗普日前批评教宗良十四世后，梅洛尼为教宗发声，两人之间的裂痕进一步加深。

US President Trump denounces his former political ally, Italian Prime Minister Meloni: "She doesn't care whether or not Iran has a nuclear weapon," "She makes people unable to accept her." After Trump criticized Pope Leo XIV the other day, Meloni spoke out for the Pope, further deepening the rift between the two.

> 彭博社报道，意大利《晚邮报》星期二（4月14日）对特朗普进行了六分钟采访。特朗普在受访时说，他和梅洛尼“很久没有说话了”，这与他过去谈到梅洛尼的态度完全不同。

Bloomberg reports that Italian news daily Corriere della Sera conducted a six-minute interview on April 14th with Trump. Trump told them that he and Meloni "haven't spoken for a very long time," a completely different attitude as compared to when he spoke about Meloni in the past.

> 特朗普曾赞叹梅洛尼是“风靡欧洲””的一位“美丽年轻女子”。

Trump once admired Meloni for being "a beautiful girl," "fashionable across Europe."
