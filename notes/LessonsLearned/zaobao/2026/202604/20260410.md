Frequent consumers of fast food should be careful from liver fat

> 不喝酒，就不会患上脂肪肝？

If you don't drink alcoholic beverages, does that mean that you won't get sick due to a fatty liver?

> 未必。如果常吃快餐，可能提高患上非酒精性脂肪肝病（non-alcoholic fatty liver disease，简称NAFLD）的风险。NAFLD意指很少喝酒或完全不喝酒的人所出现的一系列肝脏病症，主要特征是肝细胞中储存的脂肪过多。NAFLD可能导致肝硬化、肝衰竭或肝癌，最坏情况是丧命。

Not necessarily. If you often eat fast food, you may raise your risk of getting a Non-alcoholic Fatty Liver Disease (NAFLD). NAFLD is a related series of liver diseases that appear among people who drink very little or completely no alcoholic beverages, and whose main characteristic is that their cells have plenty of fat deposits. NAFLD can lead to the hardening of the liver, liver failure, liver cancer, or, worse-case, death.
