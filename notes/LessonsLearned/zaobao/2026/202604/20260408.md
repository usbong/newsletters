> 心安于身找回平衡 病体当下静守安稳

Regaining balance from having peace of mind, a quiet form of stability during illness

曾庆亮医师（庆亮中医诊所）

Dr. ZENG, Qingliang (Qingliang Traditional Chinese Medicine Clinic)

> 发布/2026年4月7日 05:00

Published: 2026-04-07T05:00

## --

> 临床上常见这样的情形：有些人体检报告并不算严重，却整个人疲惫、焦虑、睡不好；也有一些人，即便面对不轻的疾病，内心仍然安定，气息反而平稳。医学所面对的，从来不只是病，更是一个人的生命状态。

This is an often observed scene at the clinic: Some people show nothing serious in their physical exams, but, as a whole, feel exhausted and anxious, and also unable to sleep. Meanwhile, there are those, who, even during consultation have a non-mild illness, are still inwardly calm, their breathing, stable. Clinical consultation is never just about the illness, but more about the state of a person's life.
