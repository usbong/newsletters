Long-term exposure to a smoking environment: Even non-smokers could get Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD)

CHEN, Yingzhen

> 发布/2026年4月21日 06:02

## --

> 空气污染是肺部问题的重要风险因素，即使不吸烟，长期处于污染环境中也可能导致慢性阻塞性肺疾病。大约三成吸烟者可能患上慢阻肺病；而患者一旦戒烟，肺泡壁的进一步损伤可被有效减缓，因此戒烟十分关键。

Air pollution is a critical risk factor for lung problems. Even if a person doesn't smoke, long-term exposure to a smoking environment can also lead to Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD). About 30% of smokers could develop COPD. Patients who've quit smoking can successfully slow down damage to the alveolar walls of their lungs, such that quitting smoking is crucial to solving the problem.
