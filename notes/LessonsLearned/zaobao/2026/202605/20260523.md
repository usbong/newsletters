> IBM获10亿美元拨款 股价应声大涨

IBM gets 1 billion USD in grants, its stock price surges in response

> 王阳发

Yangfa WANG

> 发布/2026年5月22日 23:46

Published: 2026-05-18T05:01

## --

> 国际商业机器公司（IBM）星期四（5月21日）股价飙升12.43%之后，星期五（22日）美国开盘至今继续上涨2.33%至257.73美元。因美国特朗普政府已同意向IBM拨款10亿美元（12亿8000万新元），用于建设量子计算晶片代工厂。

This Thursday (May 21), the stock price of International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) soared 12.43%, and on Friday (May 22) when the US opened, up until now, it continued to rise by 2.33% to 257.73 USD owing to the fact that the Trump administration has agreed to give IBM a grant of 1 billion USD for use with building a quantum computer semiconductor factory.

> 美国旨在巩固在新兴行业领域的领导地位，拨款给IBM是这项广泛战略的一部分。

The US aims to solidify its leadership position in emerging industries, and giving this grant to IBM is part of its broad strategy.

> IBM星期四在一份声明中说，公司还将投资10亿美元成立一家名为Anderon的新公司，负责生产量子处理器。

IBM said in a statement on Thursday that the company will also be investing 1 billion USD to establish a new company called Anderon, which will be responsible for producing quantum processors.
