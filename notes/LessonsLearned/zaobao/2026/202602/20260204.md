> 社交不是奢侈 是整体健康必需品

Social interaction isn't a luxury, but a necessity in holistic health

> 陈映蓁

CHEN, Yingzhen

> 发布/2026年2月3日 05:05

Published: 2026-02-03T05:05

## --

> 许多人谈健康，只想到体检和压力，却忽略同样关键的“社交健康”。研究显示，孤单和社会孤立会增加慢性病与心脏病风险。佳节将至，别忘向孤单的亲友，主动发个讯息、约一顿饭，不只是礼貌关心，也是守护彼此整体健康与生活质量。

Many people talk about health, thinking only about physical examinations and stress, while ignoring "social interaction," an equally crucial component in health. Research shows that loneliness and social isolation can increase the risk of chronic and cardiovascular diseases. As the festivities draw near, let us not forget our friends who're lonely by proactively sending them a message, having a meal together, not just out of a polite concern, but in order to protect each other's holistic health and quality of life.
