> 美俄同意恢复军事对话 核条约续约有望

US and Russia agree to resume military dialogue, renewal of Nuclear Treaty hoped

> 蔡添成

CAI, Tiancheng

> 发布/2026年2月5日 23:30

Published: 2026-02-05T23:30

## --

> （莫斯科／华盛顿综合电）美国和俄罗斯的《新削减战略武器条约》（New START）周四（2月5日）到期，但几个小时后，就有消息指美俄可能同意到期后继续遵守条约。联合国与国际组织呼吁美俄谈判限制核武军备，担心引发新的军备竞赛。

Moscow/Washington News: "New Strategic Arms Treaty: New START" between the US and Russia expired on Thursday, Feb. 5, although a few hours later, there was news indicating that the US and Russia may continue to adhere to the treaty after the expiration of the agreement. The United Nations and other international organizations have called on the US and Russia to negotiate toward limiting nuclear armaments, fearing that this may lead to a new arms race.

> 美国新闻网站Axios周四报道，熟悉谈判的消息人士称，美俄即将达成一项协议，在《新削减战略武器条约》到期后继续遵守条约。

US news site Axios reported on Thursday that sources familiar with the negotiations say that the US and Russia will soon form an agreement adhering to the "New Strategic Arms Treaty" after its expiration.

> 报道说，双方过去24小时在阿布扎比进行谈判。目前仍不确定美俄是否同意继续遵守条约多一段时间，可能多半年。

The report said that in the past 24 hours, both parties proceeded with negotiations in Abu Dhabi. Right now, it still remains uncertain whether or not the US and Russia will continue to adhere to the treaty after a period of time, possibly up to six months.
