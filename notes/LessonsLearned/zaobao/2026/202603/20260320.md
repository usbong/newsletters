> 特朗普称美以突袭伊朗如同日本当年偷袭珍珠港

Trump says the surprise attack by the US on Iran was like Japan's silent attack on Pearl Harbor

> 发布/2026年3月20日 07:53

Published: 2026-03-20T07:53

## --

> 美国总统特朗普在椭圆形办公室会晤日本首相高市早苗时，以日本偷袭珍珠港为例，为美国和以色列2月28日突袭伊朗的行动辩护。

When US President Trump met with Japan Prime Minister Sanae Takaichi at the Oval Office, he used Japan's silent attack on Pearl Harbor as an example to justify the US-Israel's surprise attack on Iran on February 28th.

> 路透社报道，美日首脑星期四（3月19日）在白宫椭圆形办公室会晤时，一名日本记者问特朗普，美国在打击伊朗前为何没有先告知日本或其他盟友。

Reuters reports that when the heads from both the US and Japan met at the Oval Office in the White House on Thursday (March 19), a Japanese reporter asked Trump why there was no prior notification to Japan and other allies before the US attacked Iran.

> 特朗普说：“我们行动非常果断，而且没有事先通知任何人，因为我们想要出其不意攻其不备。”

Trump said: "Our actions are very decisive, and we didn't first notify anybody about it prior, because we wanted to attack unexpectedly and when unprepared."
