> 通过Roblox接触极端思想 马国六青少年密谋袭警局宗教场所被捕

Six Malaysian youths arrested for plotting to attack police stations and religious sites after getting hold of extremist ideas through Roblox

> 发布/2026年3月6日 18:50

Published: 2026-03-06T18:50

> 更新/2026年3月6日 22:27

Updated: 2026-03-06T22:27

## --

>（吉隆坡综合讯）马来西亚警方逮捕六名涉嫌参与恐怖主义活动的青少年。调查显示，他们通过电子游戏Roblox和社交媒体接触极端内容，甚至密谋购买武器，对警局、宗教场所、政府重要设施及其他公共场所发动袭击。

(Kuala Lumpur News) Malaysian police authorities have arrested six youths involved in terrorist activities. According to investigations, they got hold of extremist content through social media and the digital game, Roblox, going so far as to plan buying weapons and launching an attack on police stations, religious sites, important government facilities and public spaces.

> 《星洲日报》报道，马国警察总长莫哈末卡立星期五（3月6日）在记者会上说，警方根据情报于2月14日至15日，在吉隆坡、柔佛、吉打及登嘉楼等地展开行动，逮捕六名年龄介于16岁至21岁的青少年，其中三人未满18岁。他们将于下周被控上庭。

According to Sin Chew Daily, the head of the Malaysian Police, Chief Mohd Khalid, said during a press conference on Friday (March 6) that police authorities have begun operations in Kuala Lumpur, Johor, Kedah, Terengganu and others, in accordance with the reports from February 14 to 15, arresting six youths between the ages of 16 and 21, three of whom are under 18 years old. They will be prosecuted in court next week.  

> 警方调查发现，他们最初是在Roblox和社媒等平台接触到极端组织“伊斯兰国”（IS）的宣传和极端思想，随后加入WhatsApp、Telegram和Discord等平台的相关群组，其中一些群组有成员上千人。

Police investigations have found that they began to get hold of extremist ideas and propaganda from the extremist organization "Islamic State (IS)" in Roblox and social media platforms. Soon afterward, they joined related groups in WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord and others, some of which have thousands of members.
