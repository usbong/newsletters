> 肥胖带来连锁伤害 心梗中风癌症风险升高

Obesity brings chains of harms, increasing the risk of heart attack, stroke and cancer

Prof. CHEN, Huaiqin, Senior Consultant at the National University Heart Centre in Singapore

> 发布/2026年3月10日 05:01

## --

> 肥胖也可导致心衰，尤其是舒张性心衰，以及房颤等心律失常和睡眠呼吸暂停综合征。更有科学数据显示，肥胖正超越吸烟成为致癌的可预防因素。

Obesity can also lead to heart failure, especially diastolic heart failure, as well as atrial fibrillation,  arrhythmia, sleep apnea and other syndromes. Further, scientific data show that obesity is now surpassing smoking as a preventable risk factor against cancer.
