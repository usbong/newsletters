[Video] French President Macron in formal western attire and China's Ping-pong Team showcase their ping-pong skills 

> 发布/2025年12月6日 09:58

## --

> 法国总统马克龙星期五（12月5日）访问四川成都期间，到四川省体育馆看望参加混团世界杯比赛的法国乒乓球运动员，并与法国队球员一起同中国乒乓球运动员切磋球技。

French President Macron visited Chengdu, Sichuan last Friday (12/5). He went to the Sichuan Gym to witness France's ping-pong team participate in the mixed team World Cup. Together with the French team, he showcased his ping-pong skills to China's players.

> 现场视频可见，马克龙身穿西装、皮鞋，与法国球员一起，同中国乒乓球运动员王楚钦、孙颖莎、林诗栋等挥拍较量，并与中国乒协主席王励勤进行互动。

As can be seen in the video, President Macron was wearing western clothing and leather shoes. He was with the French team, as they played ping-pong with China's players, WANG, Chuqin, SUN, Yingsha, LIN, Shidong and others. He also interacted with WANG, Liqin, China's Table Tennis Association President.

赛后，王励勤向马克龙赠送球队队服。

After the contest, WANG, Liqin gifted President Macron with their ping-pong team's uniform.
