> 高市入住首相府 亲自照顾中风丈夫成话题

Ms. Takaichi moves to the Prime Minister's Office, Having to personally take care of her stroke-stricken husband could be an issue

> 符祝慧东京特派员

Special Correspondent in Tokyo: FU, Zhuhui

> 发布/2025年12月29日 22:03

Published: 2025-12-29T22:03

## --

> 日本首相高市早苗年末乔迁首相府开始新生活，外界关心她能否国事与家事兼顾。日本媒体引述高市身边人士透露，她有着深刻的家庭隐忧，除应对繁忙的政务，还得亲自照顾中风的丈夫。

Japanese Prime Minister Sanae Takaichi moves to the Prime Minister's office by the end of the year to start her new life as PM. The public is worried that she might not be able to take care of the nation and her family together. A person close to the PM has revealed to Japanese media that Ms. Takaichi is currently struggling with a family secret: Aside from her busy life working in government, she still has to personally take care of her ailing husband who's been stricken by stroke.
