> 运动了血糖不降反升？可能顺序不对

Does your blood glucose level go up instead of go down even after exercising? Maybe the order by which your doing the exercises isn't right

> 李亦筠

LI, Yiyun

> 发布/2025年12月26日 05:00

Published: 2025-12-26T05:00

## --

> 不少人一提到控糖，第一反应往往是“该怎么吃”，却忽略了运动方式其实同样关键。有些人甚至纳闷：明明已经去运动了，血糖怎么不降反升？对此，台湾糖尿病卫教师（Diabetes Educator）林冠怡在脸书分享，这样的情况并不罕见，关键往往出在运动的类型与进行顺序。

Not a few people who've been told to control their sugar levels often have as their first reaction: "What then should I eat?" But ignoring the way by which the exercises are done is actually equally important. Some people even wonder, <i>I've already been exercising, yet how come my blood glucose level goes up instead of down?</i> With regard to this, Taiwanese Diabetes Educator LIN, Guanyi shares on Facebook that this type of predicament isn't rare. The key, he says, is frequently found in the type of exercise and the order by which these exercises are done.
