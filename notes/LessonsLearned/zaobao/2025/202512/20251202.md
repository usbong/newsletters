> 英国同意提高药价 美英达成药品零关税协议

England agrees to raising medicine prices, US-UK reach zero tariff agreement for medicines

> 发布/2025年12月2日 07:07

Published: 2025-12-02T07:07

## --

> 美国和英国宣布达成药品零关税协议，英国同意增加药物支出并改革药品定价机制，以换取美国允许英国生产药品及医疗技术免关税进口。 

England and the United States have announced that they've reached a zero-tariff agreement for medicines. England will be increasing its medicine expenditures and revising its set pricing for medicines in exchange for the United States allowing England to produce these medicines itself as well as giving England tariff exemptions when it imports technology used for medical treatment.
