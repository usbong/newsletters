> 汉堡王中国易主 CPE源峰将持股约83%

Burger King China transfers ownership, CPE YuanFeng gets about 83% of the shares

> 发布/2025年11月11日 22:43

Published: 2025-11-11T22:43

## --

>（上海综合讯）继全球咖啡连锁巨头星巴克交出中国业务控股权之后，美国快餐连锁品牌汉堡王的中国业务也出售给中国资方。

(Shanghai News) Following the relinquishing of international coffee chain giant Starbucks' control over its business in China, U.S. fast-food chain brand Burger King has also sold its China business to Chinese investors.

> 综合路透社与《证券时报》报道，资产管理机构CPE源峰与汉堡王星期一（11月10日）达成战略合作，双方将成立合资企业“汉堡王中国”。

Reuters reports that asset management firm CPE YuanFeng and Burger King have reached a strategic partnership agreement last November 10, in which both parties agree to establish the joint venture "Burger King China."

> 根据协议，CPE源峰将向汉堡王中国注入3.5亿美元（4.56亿新元）的初始资金，用于支持餐厅门店扩张、市场营销、菜单创新以及运营能力的提升。交易完成后，CPE源峰将持有汉堡王中国约83%股权，餐饮品牌国际集团（RBI）将保留约17%股权。

Based on the agreement, CPE YuanFeng will infuse an initial capital of 3.5 billion USD into Burger King China, with the goal of using the money in order to support the store's expansion, marketing, menu innovation, as well as the raising of its business capabilities. Once the transaction completes, CPE YuanFeng will own about 83% of Burger King China, while Restaurant Brands International Inc. (RBI) will keep about 17%.
