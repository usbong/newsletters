> Nintendo demanda al gobierno de Estados Unidos por los aranceles impuestos por Donald Trump

Nintendo sues the US government for the tariffs imposed by Donald Trump

> Exige su devolución íntegra "con intereses".

The company demands a full refund "with interest."

> Nintendo of America ha demandado al gobierno de Estados Unidos con motivo de los aranceles impuestos por el presidente Donald Trump el año pasado.

Nintendo of America has sued the US government due to the tariffs imposed by President Donald Trump last year.

> Los aranceles, una medida tomada por Donald Trump supuestamente para equilibrar la balanza comercial entre Estados Unidos y el resto de países, fueron declarados ilegales por parte del Tribunal Supremo hace unas semanas, en el mayor varapalo judicial - por el momento - de la segunda legislatura del polémico presidente norteamericano.

The tariffs, a measure taken by Donald Trump to supposedly create a trade balance between the United States and the rest of the countries, were declared illegal by the Supreme Court a few weeks ago, in the biggest judicial setback―so far―during the second legislature of the controversial North American president.

> En la demanda, presentada hoy mismo por el equipo legal de Nintendo of America, se alega que Trump ha implementado órdenes ejecutivas "ilegales" que "imponen aranceles en importaciones de una amplia cantidad de países".

It was presented in the lawsuit by Nintendo of America's legal team today itself that Trump is alleged to have implemented "illegal" executive orders that "impose importation tariffs on a broad number of countries."

> La compañía, por lo tanto, exige que le sea devuelto el importe completo pagado en concepto de esos aranceles "con intereses", alegando que "Nintendo of America tiene derecho a demandar al ser el importador de bienes que están sujetos a las IEEPA Duties" y que la compañía "ha sufrido un perjuicio" por culpa de esos aranceles.

The company, therefore, is demanding to get back the full amount that it had paid due to these tariffs "with interest," alleging that "Nintendo of America has the right to be the importer of goods that are subject to IEEPA Duties" and that the company "has suffered damages" because of the said tariffs.
