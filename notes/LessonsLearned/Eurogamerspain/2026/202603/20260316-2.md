> Digital Sun ha anunciado ReVamp para PC.

Digital Sun has announced ReVamp for PC.

> El estudio valenciano responsable de Moonlighter y Cataclismo está trabajando en un tower defense con elementos roguelite protagonizado por Drácula.

The Valencian studio responsible for Moonlighter and Cataclismo is working on a tower defense with roguelite elements and starred by Dracula.

> Nuestra tarea es diseñar poco a poco nuestra fortaleza inexpugnable colocando diferentes salas para derrotar a los humanos que tratan de matar al vampiro, al que podremos manejar directamente para defender el castillo. 

Our task is to gradually design our impregnable fortress by placing different rooms in certain areas of the castle in order to defeat the humans who're trying to kill the vampire, whom we'll be able to directly control to defend the castle.

> La muerte no será el final, ya que la diosa de la noche Achlys nos ha condenado a reconstruir eternamente el castillo. La parte roguelite incluye desbloqueos de nuevos tipos de habitación, nuevas unidades y nuevos poderes para el propio Drácula.

Death won't be the end, given that the goddess of the night, Achlys, has condemned us to reconstruct the castle for eternity. The roguelite part includes unlocking new types of rooms, new units and new powers for Dracula himself.

> Digital Sun está trabajando también en Moonlighter 2, que está en acceso anticipado en PC y llegará en 2026 a Switch 2. Aquí puedes leer nuestras [impresiones del Early Access de Moonlighter 2](https://www.eurogamer.es/moonlighter-2-the-endless-vault-impresiones-early-access).

Digital Sun is also working on Moonlighter 2, which is in Early Access on PC and will be coming to Switch 2 in 2026. Here you can read our [impressions of the Early Access of Moonlighter 2](https://www.eurogamer.es/moonlighter-2-the-endless-vault-impresiones-early-access).

> ReVamp está en desarrollo para PC. Aún no tiene ventana de lanzamiento aproximada. 

ReVamp is in development for PC. It still doesn't have an approximate release date.
