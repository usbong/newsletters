> Crunchyroll y Kadokawa han publicado un nuevo tráiler de la serie de animación Sekiro: No Defeat.

Crunchyroll and Kadokawa have published a new trailer of the animation series Sekiro: No Defeat.

> Este anime producido por Qzil.la (Enter The Garden, Kuromi’s Pretty Journey) y ARCH (Promare, Tribe Nine), basado en el videojuego de FromSoftware (Dark Souls, Elden Ring), se anunció en junio del año pasado.

The anime produced by Qzil.la (Enter The Garden, Kuromi’s Pretty Journey) and ARCH (Promare, Tribe Nine), based on the video from FromSoftware (Dark Souls, Elden Ring), was announced in June of last year.

> La serie seguirá de cerca la trama del videojuego, ambientado en una versión fantástica del cierre del periodo Sengoku (finales del siglo XVI) y protagonizado por un shinobi apodado Lobo. 

The series closely follows the plot of the video game, set in a fantastic version during the close of the Sengoku period (by the end of 16th century) and led by a shinobi nicknamed Wolf.

> En el primer anuncio se confirmó que varios actores del juego recuperarían sus papeles en la serie: Daisuke Namikawa como Lobo, Miyuki Satou como Kuro y Kenjiro Tsuda como Genichiro Ashina. En este segundo anuncio se han confirmado varios actores de doblaje adicionales del juego:

In the first announcement, it was confirmed that several actors of the game would be getting back their roles in the series: Daisuke Namikawa as Wolf, Miyuki Satou as Kuro and Kenjiru Tsuda as Genichiro Ashina. In this second announcement, several actors for the additional dubbing of the game have been confirmed.

> El tema principal de la serie será “Blu”, del fallecido compositor Ryuichi Sakamoto, responsable de bandas sonoras de películas como El Último Emperador o El Renacido, además de ser el responsable del sonido de inicio de la consola Sega Dreamcast.

The main theme of the series will be "Blue," from the deceased composer Ryuichi Sakamoto, responsible for the soundtrack of films like The Last Emperor and The Reborn, in addition to being responsible for the startup sound of the Sega Dreamcast console.

> Sekiro: No Defeat se estrenará en 2026 a través de Crunchyroll.
