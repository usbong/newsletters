## --

> Limited Run Games ha anunciado la fecha de lanzamiento de Marvel MaXimum Collection.

Limited Run Games has announced the release date of Marvel MaXimum Collection.

> El recopilatorio para PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch y PC desarrollado en colaboración con Marvel Games y Konami, en el cual se incluyen seis videojuegos clásicos de consolas y recreativa, en trece versiones distintas en total, estará disponible a partir del día 27 de marzo en formato digital.

The collection for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch and PC developed in collaboration with Marvel Games and Konami, in which six classic video games for consoles and arcade have been added for a total of 13 different versions, will be available in digital format starting March 27.

> Los títulos incluidos son X-Men: The Arcade Game (Arcade), Captain America and The Avengers (Arcade, Mega Drive, NES), Spider-Man/Venom: Maximum Carnage (Mega Drive, SNES), Venom/Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety (Mega Drive, SNES), Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade’s Revenge (Mega Drive, SNES, Game Boy, Game Gear) y Silver Surfer (NES).

The titles included are X-Men: The Arcade Game (Arcade), Captain America and The Avengers (Arcade, Mega Drive, NES), Spider-Man/Venom: Maximum Carnage (Mega Drive, SNES), Venom/Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety (Mega Drive, SNES), Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade's Revenge (Mega Drive, SNES, Game Boy, Game Gear) and Silver Surfer (NES).

> Aparte de mejoras de calidad vida, como la opción de guardar la partida en cualquier momento o de rebobinar, también se incluirán distinto filtros de imagen, un archivo con scans de instrucciones, cajas y anuncios de revista, un reproductor de música con las canciones de todos los juegos, un museo, un menú de trucos y rollback netcode para X-Men: The Arcade Game. 

Other than quality of life improvements like the option to save or rewind the game at anytime, various image filters, an archive with the scans of the instructions, boxes and magazine ads, a music player with all the songs in the games, a museum, a cheat menu and rollback netcode for X-Men: The Arcade Game will also be included.
