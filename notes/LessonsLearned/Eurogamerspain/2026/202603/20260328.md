> NIS lanzará en otoño Village in the Shade, un juego de granjas con elementos de terror del equipo de Yomawari

NIS is launching this fall Village in the Shade, a farming game with elements of horror from the team behind Yomawari

> Se lanzará en PC, PlayStation 5, Switch y Switch 2.

It's getting released on PC, PlayStation 5, Switch and Switch 2.

> NIS America ha confirmado el lanzamiento en occidente de Village in the Shade.

NIS America has confirmed the western release of Village in the Shade

> Se trata del próximo juego del equipo interno de Nippon Ichi Software responsable de la trilogía de juegos de terror Yomawari, liderado por Yu Mizokami.

It's the upcoming game from Nippon Ichi Software's internal team, the same one responsible for Yomawari, the horror trilogy game led by Yu Mizokami.

> Aunque en la superficie parece un juego de granjas al estilo Story of Seasons o Stardew Valley con la particular estética de la serie Yomawari, pronto comprobamos que el idílico pueblecito mantiene un vínculo con el terror. 

While on the surface, it appears to be a farming game in the style of Story of Seasons and Stardew Valley that is accompanied by the distinct aesthetic of the Yomawari series, we soon found out that the game's idyllic town keeps a link to the horror genre.
