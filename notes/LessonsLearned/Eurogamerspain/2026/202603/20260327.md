> El roguelike Hades 2 llegará a PlayStation 5 y Xbox Series X/S en abril

Roguelike Hades 2 is coming to PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S in April

> Estará disponible de lanzamiento en Game Pass.

It'll be available upon release on Game Pass.

> Supergiant Games ha confirmado plataformas adicionales para Hades 2.

Supergiant Games has confirmed additional platforms for Hades 2.

> El roguelike del estudio responsable de Bastion, Transistor y Pyre se publicó en PC y consolas de Nintendo en septiembre de 2025 tras año y medio en acceso anticipado.

The roguelike from the studio responsible for Bastian, Transistor and Pyre was published on PC and Nintendo's consoles in September 2025 after a year and a half in Early Access.

> En esta secuela de Hades manejamos a Melínoe, hermana menor de Zagreo, en su cruzada contra el mismísimo Cronos después de que se alce contra sus descendientes. 

In this Hades sequel, we take control of Melínoe, sister of Zagreo, in a crusade against the very same Cronos himself after he rose up against his descendants.
