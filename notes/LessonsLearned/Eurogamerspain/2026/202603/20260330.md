> Stranger than Heaven, lo nuevo de los creadores de Yakuza, se ambientará en cinco ciudades de Japón entre 1915 y 1965

Stranger than Heaven, the new game from the creators of Yakuza, will be taking place in five cities in Japan between 1915 and 1965

We'll see more during an Xbox Live in May 6.

> Sega ha confirmado nuevos detalles de Stranger than Heaven y ha publicado un tráiler con escenas de gameplay.

Sega has confirmed new details of Stranger than Heaven and has published a trailer with gameplay footages.

> El nuevo juego de Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio, responsable de la saga Yakuza/Like a Dragon, se presentó en diciembre de 2024 durante la gala de The Game Awards, todavía con el nombre en clave Project Century.

The new game from Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio, responsible for the Yakuza/Like a Dragon series, was presented in December 2024 during the The Game Awards gala night, still having the code name Project Century.

> La trama nos llevará al mundo criminal de cinco ciudades japonesas a lo largo de cinco épocas diferentes (1915, 1929, 1943, 1951 y 1965), pero apenas conocemos aún detalles sobre la historia. 

The plot will take us to the criminal world of five Japanese cities throughout five different eras (1915, 1929, 1943, 1951 and 1965), though we hardly yet know the details of the story.
