> Limited Run Games ha anunciado Marvel MaXimum Collection.

Limited Run Games has announced Marvel MaXimum Collection.

> Se trata de un recopilatorio para PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch y PC desarrollado en colaboración con Marvel Games y Konami, en el cual se incluyen seis videojuegos clásicos de consolas y recreativa, en trece versiones distintas en total.

It's a collection for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch and PC developed in collaboration with Marvel Games and Konami, in which six classic console and arcade games are included, for a total of thirteen different versions.

> Los títulos incluidos son X-Men: The Arcade Game (Arcade), Captain America and The Avengers (Arcade, Mega Drive, NES), Spider-Man/Venom: Maximum Carnage (Mega Drive, SNES), Venom/Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety (Mega Drive, SNES), Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade’s Revenge (Mega Drive, SNES, Game Boy, Game Gear) y Silver Surfer (NES).

The titles included are X-Men: The Arcade Game (Arcade), Captain America and The Avengers (Arcade, Mega Drive, NES), Spider-Man/Venom: Maximum Carnage (Mega Drive, SNES), Venom/Spider-Man: Separation Anxiety (Mega Drive, SNES), Spider-Man/X-Men: Arcade's Revenge (Mega Drive, SNES, Game Boy, Game Gear) and Silver Surfer (NES).

> Aparte de mejoras de calidad vida, como la opción de guardar la partida en cualquier momento o de rebobinar, también se incluirán distinto filtros de imagen, un archivo con scans de instrucciones, cajas y anuncios de revista, un reproductor de música con las canciones de todos los juegos, un museo, un menú de trucos y rollback netcode para X-Men: The Arcade Game.

Aside from quality of life improvements, like the option to save the game at any moment or to rewind, different image filters will also be added, a file with scans of the game instructions, boxes and magazine ads, a music player with songs from all the games, a museum, a menu for in-game cheats and rollback netcode for X-Men: The Arcade Game.

> Todavía no se ha concretado la fecha de lanzamiento de Marvel MaXimum Collection, pero se espera que sea este mismo año 2026. 

The release date of Marvel MaXimum Collection isn't yet specified, but it's hoped that it would be this same year, 2026.
