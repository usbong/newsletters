> Ubisoft ha presentado las novedades del Año 11 de Rainbow Six Siege.

Ubisoft has presented the new features that Rainbow Six Siege is getting for Year 11.

> El shooter multijugador recibirá el 3 de marzo la primera temporada de su undécimo año con la llegada de Operation Silent Hunt, que añadirá a Solid Snake de la serie Metal Gear Solid como agente atacante. Contará con la TACIT .45 (pistola semiautomática son silenciador) y gadgets pensados para obtener información del enemigo como el Radar Soliton Mk III.

The multiplayer shooter will be celebrating the first season of its 11th year on March 3rd with the arrival of Operation Silent Hunt, which will add Solid Snake from the Metal Gear Solid series as an attack agent. It'll include the TACIT .45 (semiautomatic pistol with silencer) as well as gadgets thought to be used to obtain information from the enemy like the Radar Soliton Mk III.

> David Hayter pondrá voz al agente, aunque también se lanzarán skins de Gray Fox (Jackal) y Meryl (Ash). Snake llegará junto a un modo temporal 4v4 que unirá a Zero (Sam Fisher) con Snake y una misión para Dual Front con homenajes a Metal Gear Solid. Sin embargo, tras esta temporada, se retirará el modo Dual Front.

David Hayter will be putting the voice to the agent, although there will also be skins for Gray Fox (Jackal) and Meryl (Ash) that will be available during the launch. Snake will be arriving together with a 4v4  mode that will bring Zero (Sam Fisher) and Snake together, as well as a mission for Dual Front that pays homage to Metal Gear Solid. However, after this season, the Dual Front mode will be retired.

> Durante las dos primeras semanas de la Temporada 1 se lanzará un nuevo modo 1v1 a Arcade y se actualizará el aspecto de los mapas Oregon, Coastline y Villa.

During the first two seasons of Season 1, a new 1v1 Arcade mode will be launched and will update parts of the Oregon, Coastline and Villa maps.

> Las temporadas 2, 3 y 4 se centrarán en mejoras en el gameplay y tres mapas modernizados por temporada. En PC se integrará el antitrampas con inicio seguro con el sistema "R6 ShieldGuard Secure".

Seasons 2, 3 and 4 will center around the improvements in the gameplay and the three maps modernized for the season. On PC, anti-cheats with secure start using the "R6 ShieldGuard Secure" system are getting integrated into the game.

> La Temporada 2 traerá cambios al agente Dokkaebi y un nuevo mapa llamado Calypso Casino, inspirado por Rainbow Six Vegas. La Temporada 3 traerá un nuevo agente de defensa y un sistema de progresión a largo plazo para subir nivel a todos los agentes de manera independiente, además de un modo arcade 3v3 y una cola en solitario para los jugadores de alto nivel competitivo.

Season 2 will bring changes to agent Dokkaebi and a new map called Calypso Casino, inspired by Rainbow Six Vegas. Season 3 will bring a new defense agent and a long-term progression system to raise the level of all the agents individually, in addition to a 3v3 Arcade mode and a single player mode for those who play competitively and at high levels.

> Con la Temporada 4 llegará un hub social que permitirá mandar invitaciones y pasar tiempo con amigos independientemente de su plataforma.

With Season 4, there'll be a social hub that will allow the sending of invites and the hanging out with friends independent of the platform.
