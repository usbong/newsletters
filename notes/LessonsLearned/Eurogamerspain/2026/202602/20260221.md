> Anunciado un Pokémon Presents para la semana que viene

Pokémon Presents is announced for the coming week

> Coincidiendo con el trigésimo aniversario de la franquicia.

Coinciding with the franchise's 30th anniversary.

> Noticias por [Josep Maria Sempere](https://www.eurogamer.es/authors/josep-maria-sempere), Director

News by [Josep Maria Sempere](https://www.eurogamer.es/authors/josep-maria-sempere), Editor-in-Chief

> Publicado el 20 de febrero de 2026

Published on February 20, 2026

## --

> The Pokémon Company ha anunciado la emisión de un nuevo Pokémon Presents.

The Pokémon Company has announced the broadcasting of a new Pokémon Presents.

> El evento se podrá seguir a través de los canales oficiales en YouTube y Twitch, y se emitirá a las 15:00 hora peninsular del viernes, 27 de febrero.

The event will be available through its official channels on YouTube and Twitch, and will be broadcasted on Friday, February 27, at 22:00 (Philippine Time).

> Esta fecha coincide, además, con el trigésimo aniversario de la franquicia Pokémon.

The date, in addition, is coinciding with the Pokémon franchise's 30th anniversary.

> "Para celebrar esta fiesta, The Pokémon Company Group invita a los entrenadores de todo el mundo a ver una presentación en vídeo Pokémon Presents en el Pokémon Day para emocionantes actualizaciones y anuncios de toda la franquicia Pokémon".

"In order to celebrate the festival, The Pokémon Company Group is inviting trainers from around the world to view a Pokémon Presents video presentation during the Pokémon Day to get exciting updates and announcements on everything about the Pokémon franchise."

> Ese mismo día, cuando termine el Pokémon Presents, se podrán comprar en la eShop de Nintendo Switch y Nintendo Switch 2 los clásicos Pokémon Rojo Fuego y Pokémon Verde Hoja, con un precio de 19,99€ cada uno. 

During that same day, right after the Pokémon Presents ends, the two Pokémon classics, Pokémon FireRed and Pokémon LeafGreen, will become available for purchase on the eShop of the Nintendo Switch and the Nintendo Switch 2 with a price tag of 19.99 € (1,279.36 PHP) each.
