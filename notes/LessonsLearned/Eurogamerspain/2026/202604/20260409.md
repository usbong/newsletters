> Gameloft ha anunciado oficialmente Nickelodeon Extreme Tennis: Next!.

Gameloft has officially announced Nickelodeon Extreme Tennis: Next!.

> Se trata de un juego de tenis desarrollado por el estudio Old Skull Games, que tendrá un precio de 29,99€ y contará con versiones para PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch y PC a partir del próximo día 28 de mayo.

It's a tennis game developed by Old Skull Games and will have a price tag of 29.99 euros (approx. 1,919.36 PHP) and will get versions for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch and PC starting this May 28.

> Nickelodeon Extreme Tennis: Next! contará con quince personajes de franquicias como SpongeBob SquarePants, Avatar: The Last Airbender o Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, más de quinientos trajes y accesorios para personalizar a nuestro personaje y habilidades únicas para "dar la vuelta al partido".

Nickelodeon Extreme Tennis: Next! will have 15 characters from various franchises like SpongeBob SquarePants, Avatar: The Last Airbender and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, over 500 costumes and accessories to customize our character and unique abilities to "turn the game around."

> Respecto a modos de juego, se prometen tanto partidos clásicos como minijuegos, un modo Historia, un modo Torneo y multijugador uno contra uno o cooperativo a pantalla partida. 

With respect to the game modes, Nickelodeon has promised classic games like mini games, a Story mode, a Tournament mode as well as a one-on-one or cooperative split-screen multiplayer option.
