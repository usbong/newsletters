> Paramount Pictures ha publicado el primer tráiler de la nueva adaptación cinematográfica de Street Fighter.

Paramount Pictures has published the first trailer of the new cinematographic adaptation of Street Fighter.

> La popular saga de juegos de lucha de Capcom volverá a la gran pantalla el próximo 16 de octubre de 2026, en una cinta dirigida por Kitao Sakurai (The Eric Andre Show, Bad Trip) a partir de un guion escrito por Dalan Musson (Captain America: Brave New World, Sky: The Coming Race).

The popular fighting game series from Capcom will be coming to the big screen on October 16, 2026, in a film directed by Kitao Sakurai (The Eric Andre Show, Bad Trip) based on a script written by Dalan Musson (Captain America: Brave New World, Sky: The Coming Race).

> En cuanto al reparto, cuenta con Jason Momoa (Blanka), David Dastmalchian (M. Bison), Noah Centineo (Ken), Andrew Koji (Ryu), 50 Cent (Balrog), Callina Liang (Chun-Li), Cody Rhodes (Guile) o Joe Anoa'i (Akuma), entre otros).

Regarding the casting, it'll include Jason Momoa (Blanka), David Dastmalchian (M. Bison), Noah Centineo (Ken), Andrew Koji (Ryu), 50 Cent (Balrog), Callina Liang (Chun-Li), Cody Rhodes (Guile) and Joe Anoa'i (Akuma), among others.

> Street Fighter ya fue adaptada - con resultados más que discutibles - en 1994, en una cinta dirigida por Steven E. de Souza (guionista de La Jungla de Cristal y 48 Horas) que contó con Jean-Claude Van Damme, Raul Julia, Kylie Minogue y Wes Studi en su reparto. 

Street Fighter was previously adapted―with more questionable results―in 1994, in a film directed by Steven E. de Souza (scriptwriter of The Crystal Jungle and 48 Hours), which included Jean-Claude Van Damme, Raul Julia, Kylie Minogue and Wes Studi in the cast.
