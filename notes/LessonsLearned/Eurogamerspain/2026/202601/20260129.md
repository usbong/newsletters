> Amazon confirma una oleada de despidos que afectará a 16.000 empleados

Amazon confirms a wave of layoffs that will affect 16,000 employees

> La segunda en apenas dos meses.

The second in barely two months.

## --

> Amazon ha anunciado una nueva oleada de despidos, la segunda en apenas un par de meses.

Amazon has announced a new wave of layoffs, the second in barely a couple of months.

> En esta ocasión, tal y como confirmaba la compañía norteamericana, afectará a 16.000 empleados en todo el mundo.

This time, as had been confirmed by the North American company, the layoffs will affect 16,000 employees all over the world.

> La vice presidenta Beth Galetti explicaba que el objetivo de esta medida es seguir "reduciendo capas y eliminar burocracia". Esta excusa es la misma que se puso en octubre, cuando 14.000 personas, de departamentos como recursos humanos, operaciones, dispositivos o Amazon Web Services, perdieron su puesto de trabajo.

Vice President Beth Galetti had explained that the objective of this measure is to continue to reduce or eliminate excess layers of bureaucracy." In fact, this is the same excuse that was given in October, when 14,000 people from departments such as human resources, operations, devices and Amazon Web Services had lost their jobs.

> Por el momento se desconoce cuáles son los departamentos afectados ahora, pero todo apunta a los de ventas, recursos humanos, Amazon Web Services y Prime Video.

While it's not known which departments will be affected at the moment, everyone's pointing toward sales, human resources, Amazon Web Services and Prime Video.

> Aunque no parece que esté ligado con esta oleada de despidos, hoy también hemos conocido que Christoph Hartmann, el máximo responsable de Amazon Game Studios (y anteriormente de 2K Games) abandonaba su puesto como parte del reenfoque de Amazon en el servicio de streaming Luna en detrimento de los juegos tradicionales de PC y consola. 

Although it doesn't seem that this other news might be tied to the wave of layoffs, we've also learned today that Christoph Hartmann, the person having the highest position at Amazon Game Studios (and, previously, at 2K Games), had left his post as part of Amazon's refocusing on its streaming service Luna to the unfortunate detriment of traditional PC and console games.
