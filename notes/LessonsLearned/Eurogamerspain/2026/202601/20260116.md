> Yakuza Kiwami 3 tendrá doce juegos de Game Gear

## --

> Sega ha desvelado que Yakuza Kiwami 3 & Dark Ties incluirá la opción de jugar a doce títulos de la clásica consola portátil Game Gear.

Sega has revealed that Yakuza Kiwami 3 & Dark Ties will include the option to play a dozen titles of the classic Game Gear portable console.

> La saga tradicionalmente había permitido a los jugadores visitar salones recreativos con máquinas clásicas de Sega como Out Run, Super Hang-On, Space Harrier, Fantasy Zone o Virtua Fighter 2, por ejemplo.

The series traditionally had allowed players to visit the arcades that housed classic Sega coin-op machines like, for example, Out Run, Super Hang-On, Space Harrier, Fantasy Zone and Virtua Fighter 2.

> Con el spin-off Lost Judgment, sin embargo, el Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio fue un paso más allá incluyendo una Master System en el despacho de Takayuki Yagami, en la cual podíamos jugar a ocho juegos. Más tarde, en Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name y Like Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii, se volvió a incluir una Master System con una selección de juegos.

With the spin-off, Lost Judgment, however, Ryu Ga Gotoku Studio was a step further by including a Master System in Takayuki Yagami's office, in which we could play eight games. Much later, in Like a Dragon Gaiden: The Man Who Erased His Name and Like a Dragon: Pirate Yakuza in Hawaii, a Master System with a catalog of games were added.

> Para Yakuza Kiwami 3 los desarrolladores han incluido una Game Gear, en la cual Kazuma Kiryu podrá jugar a doce juegos, ocho de sega (Columns, Fantasy Zone Gear, The GG Shinobi, G-LOC, Puyo Puyo, Sonic Chaos, Sonic Drift, Streets of Rage y Woody Pop) y tres de Bandai Namco (Galaga '91, Mappy y Pac-Man).

For Yakuza Kiwami 3, the developers have included a Game Gear, in which Kazuma Kiryu could play a dozen games, eight from Sega (Columns, Fantasy Zone Gear, The GG Shinobi, G-LOC, Puyo Puyo, Sonic Chaos, Sonic Drift, Streets of Rage and Woody Pop) and three from Bandai Namco (Galaga '91, Mappy and Pac-Man).

> Además, siete de estos juegos (Columns, G-LOC, Mappy, Pac-Man, Puyo Puyo, Sonic Drift y Streets of Rage) se podrán jugar en modo de dos jugadores, con un multijugador a pantalla partida.

In addition, seven of these games (Columns, G-LOC, Mappy, Pac-Man, Puyo Puyo, Sonic Drift and Streets of Rage) could be played in two player mode via a multiplayer split screen.

> Yakuza Kiwami 3 & Dark Ties, que incluye un remake del juego de PlayStation 3 más un nuevo spin-off protagonizado por Yoshitaka Mine, se pondrá a la venta el próximo 12 de febrero y contará con versiones para PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 4 y PC. 

Yakuza Kiwami 3 & Dark Ties, which includes one more new spin-off led by Yoshitaka Mine, a remake of the PlayStation 3 game, will be on sale this coming February 12 and will have versions for PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X/S, Nintendo Switch 2, PlayStation 4 and PC.
