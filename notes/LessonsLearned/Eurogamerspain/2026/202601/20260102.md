> La tienda digital GOG ha sido adquirida por Michał Kiciński, cofundador de GOG y CD Projekt

Digital Store GOG has been acquired by Michał Kiciński, co-founder of GOG and CD Projekt

> GOG ya no formará parte del estudio polaco.

GOG will no longer be part of the Polish studio.

## --

> ...Kiciński ha querido remarcar que los juegos seguirán sin tener DRM (protección digital), nuestra biblioteca será la misma tras la compra y el cliente GOG Galaxy se mantendrá opcional. De cara a 2026, quieren aumentar la participación de la comunidad con nuevas iniciativas que detallarán más adelante. 

Kiciński has wanted to reemphasize that the games will continue to exist without having DRM (digital protection), our library will still be the same after the purchase and the GOG Galaxy client will be kept optional. Come 2026, they want to increase the participation of the community in their new initiatives, which they'll be explaining in greater detail soon.
