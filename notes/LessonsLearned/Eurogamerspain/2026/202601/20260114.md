> Capcom ha anunciado la emisión de un Resident Evil Showcase.

Capcom has announced the broadcasting of a Resident Evil Showcase.

> Este evento digital se centrará en nuevo gameplay de Resident Evil Requiem, la próxima entrega principal de la saga de survival horrors.

The digital event will focus on the new Resident Evil Requiem gameplay, the next main game from the survival horror series.

> Requiem se anunció en junio de 2025 con un tráiler que mostraba a Grace Ashcroft, agente del FBI e hija de Alyssa Ashcroft (Resident Evil Outbreak), como personaje protagonista. A pesar de las numerosas filtraciones y rumores, la compañía japonesa no confirmó a Leon S. Kennedy como segundo personaje jugable hasta hace apenas un mes. 

Requiem was announced in June 2025 with a trailer that showed Grace Ashcroft, an FBI agent and the daughter of Alyssa Ashcroft (Resident Evil Outbreak), as lead character. Despite the numerous leakages and rumors, the Japanese company didn't confirm Leon S. Kennedy as the second playable character until hardly a month ago.
