> Microsoft anuncia el Xbox Developer Direct 2026, centrado en Forza Horizon 6, Fable y lo nuevo de Game Freak

> Microsoft ha anunciado un nuevo Xbox Developer Direct.

Microsoft has announced a new Xbox Developer Direct.

> Este evento se emite de forma anual desde 2023 y cuenta con entrevistas a desarrolladores de títulos de la propia Xbox Game Studios y de estudios third-party.

The event is broadcasted annually since 2023 and includes developer interviews of titles from the Xbox Game Studios itself as well as from third-party studios.

> El año pasado, por ejemplo, vimos Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, Ninja Gaiden 4, Ninja Gaiden 2 Black, South of Midnight y Doom: The Dark Ages.

Last year, for example, we saw Clair Obscur: Expedition 33, Ninja Gaiden 4, Ninja Gaiden 2 Black, South of Midnight and Doom: The Dark Ages.

> Dos de los juegos que se mostrarán en 2026 están siendo desarrollados por el estudio británico PlayGround Games: Fable y Forza Horizon 6.

Two of the games that will be shown in 2026 are being developed by the British studio, PlayGround Games: Fable and Forza Horizon 6.

> La nueva entrega de Fable recuperará la saga de RPG de Lionhead más de 16 años después de Fable 3. Durante el evento veremos nuevo material de un título que hasta ahora apenas se ha mostrado en eventos desde 2023. 

The new Fable game will bring back the Lionhead RPG series 16 years after Fable 3. During the event, we'll see new material of a title, which up to now, has hardly been shown in events since 2023.

> También veremos el primer gameplay del juego de conducción arcade Forza Horizon 6, anunciado con un breve teaser en el pasado Tokyo Game Show, que llevará el festival de conducción a Japón. 2026

We'll also see the first gameplay of the arcade driving game, Forza Horizon 6, announced with a brief teaser last Tokyo Game Show, and which will be taking the driving fever to Japan in 2026.

> Habrá hueco para un juego third party: Beast of Reincarnation, un título desarrollado por Game Freak (serie Pokémon) y publicado por Fictions, que ha heredado parte del catálogo de la difunta Private Division de Take Two.

There'll be a space for a third-party game: Beast of Reincarnation, a title developed by Game Freak (Pokémon series) and published by Fictions, a company that has inherited part of the catalog of Take Two's now defunct Private Division.

> Aunque supimos por primera vez de él en 2023, hasta mediados del año pasado no pudimos ver un gameplay, precisamente durante el Xbox Game Showcase de junio.

While we learned about this game for the first time back in 2023, until the middle part of last year, we didn't get to see any gameplay of it during the Xbox Game Showcase in June.

> El Xbox Developer Direct 2026 se emitirá el jueves 22 de enero a las 19:00 (Horario de Europa Central). 

Xbox Developer Direct 2026 will be broadcasted on Friday, January 23 at 2:00 (Philippine Time).
