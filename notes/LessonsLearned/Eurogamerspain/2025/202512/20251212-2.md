> S-GAME ha desvelado la fecha de lanzamiento de Phantom Blade Zero.

S-GAME has unveiled the release date of Phantom Blade Zero.

> El juego de acción del estudio chino es el quinto juego de este universo. El primer título fue Rainblood: Town of Death, creado con RPG Maker en 2010. Le siguieron Rainblood 2: City of Flame (2011), Rainblood Chronicles: Mirage (2013) y Phantom Blade: Executioners (2023).

The action game from the Chinese studio is the fifth game of this universe. The first title was Rainblood: Town of Death, which was created with RPG Maker in 2010. This was followed by Rainblood 2: City of Flame (2011), Rainblood Chronicles: Mirage (2013) and Phantom Blade: Executioners (2023).
